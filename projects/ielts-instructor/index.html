01 — Problem & Solution

Why this project exists

IELTS preparation has real pain points that technology can solve—but most existing tools only scratch the surface.

⚠ The Problems

⏳

Writing feedback latency

Learners submit essays but wait 1–3 days for teacher feedback. This delay kills motivation and slows the feedback loop critical for improvement.

⚖

Inconsistent scoring

Different teachers score the same essay differently. Without a standardized rubric engine, learners can't trust or compare their scores across sessions.

📦

Fragmented content

IELTS materials are scattered across dozens of websites with varying quality. No single platform offers structured, level-appropriate content with provenance tracking.

❓

No instant explanations

After a Reading test, learners see a score but don't understand why they got questions wrong. There's no per-question explanation to close the knowledge gap.

✅ The Solutions

🤖

AI-powered scoring in <5 min

Essays are scored by GPT-4o / Gemini against the official IELTS 4-criteria rubric (TR, CC, LR, GRA). Async pipeline via BullMQ delivers results through real-time SSE—no more waiting days.

📏

Standardized rubric engine

Every essay is scored with the same rubric-based system prompt + strict JSON schema validation. Consistent, comparable, reproducible scores every time.

📚

Centralized content platform

Admin-managed content with CEFR-leveled passages & prompts, publish/unpublish workflow, version tracking, and topic collections—all in one place.

💡

Instant explanations per question

Reading submissions are auto-graded instantly with per-question explanations authored by admins. Learners understand their mistakes immediately after submitting.

02 — Overview

Three roles, one ecosystem

Each role unlocks a tailored experience—from practice to management to full platform control.

Learner

  • Browse Reading passages by CEFR level (A2→C1)
  • 6 question types with instant auto-grading
  • Submit Writing essays → AI scores in <5 min
  • Dashboard with progress trends
  • Join classrooms via invite code

Instructor

  • Create classrooms with invite codes
  • Organize Topics → Lessons hierarchy
  • Link Reading passages or Writing prompts to lessons
  • View all learner submissions and progress
  • Send class-wide announcements

Admin

  • Full CRUD for passages, questions, prompts
  • Publish / unpublish content workflow
  • Content versioning with audit trail
  • User management and role changes
  • System analytics dashboard
